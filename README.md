Eco Drop: Smart Sustainable Hydration 💧Vision: To create a circular, eco-friendly hydration system that reduces plastic waste while making clean drinking water more accessible.

🌍 The Problem:

The global plastic waste crisis is driven by single-use plastic bottles.
Scale of Impact: 1.3 billion plastic bottles are sold everyday worldwide.
Inefficient Recycling: Less than 30% of these bottles are recycled; the rest end up in landfills or the oceans.
Market Gap: While consumers want sustainable alternatives, there is a distinct lack of affordable, scalable, and user-friendly refill infrastructure in urban areas.

💡 Our Solution
Eco Drop provides a tech-driven solution to eliminate single-use plastic bottles through a circular ecosystem:

Smart Infrastructure: A network of smart water refill stations across European cities.
Digital Integration: An EcoDrop mobile app and website that allows users to locate nearby stations, track their water usage, and earn eco-rewards.
Smart Hardware: A subscription-based reusable smart bottle equipped with QR/NFC scanning technology.

✨ Key Features
Gamification: Users earn points for every refill made at our stations.Data-Driven: Real-time data analytics provided to users regarding their plastic savings.
Subscription Model: An affordable, rewarding hydration service that integrates into the user's daily urban lifestyle.

🏢 Business Model
Eco Drop operates through a diversified revenue approach:
Subscription Fees: A smart bottle subscription service ($5/month).
Strategic Partnerships: Collaborations with gyms, cafés, and public venues hosting refill stations.
Sponsorships & Advertising: Revenue generated through eco-brand sponsorships and in-app advertising.
